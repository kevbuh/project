About Us

This company will be aimed towards giving contemporary small clothing companies the chance to shine without having to spend all their cash to advertise the brand. There are no viable websites nor apps that go into this market (ages 13-30). This company will not be charging costly advertising opportunities to its clients, as it will instead incentivize these brands with low funds to use the product by charging a low-cost percentage fee on the products that they sell. Brands will also have the option to buy home page advertisements based on the size and amount of time put up on the website. Clients should know that these prices will change based on the traffic flow to the company. This company will offer 1.) Platform to put out the “up and coming” clothing brands to showcase their products, 2.) A monthly subscription-based information sheet on the company for their financial status for possible investors to invest in these companies, 3.) A calendar in which customers can mark their favorite brands to see when they restock inventory.
